About AHU English Club

Our Mission

The AHU English Club was founded in 2024 with the goal of creating a supportive environment for students to improve their English language skills. We believe that language learning should be engaging, interactive, and connected to real-world communication.

Our club organizes regular activities, provides learning resources, and fosters cultural exchange between students from different backgrounds.

What We Offer

  • Weekly conversation practice with native speakers
  • Comprehensive learning materials for all levels
  • Cultural exchange events and workshops
  • Preparation for English proficiency tests
